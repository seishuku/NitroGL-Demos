Two Per-Pixel Lights with Specular Lighting

Controls:
Left Mouse+ Drag - Rotate
Right Mouse+ Drag - Move in/out

Left Mouse + Control+ Drag - Move Light 1
Right Mouse + Control + Drag - Move Light 1 in/out

Left Mouse + Shift + Drag - Move Light 2
Right Mouse + Shift + Drag - Move Light 2 in/out

T - Change textures
M - Change model
Escape - Quit
